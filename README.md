# Escape Together

Escape Together is a cooperative **split-screen puzzle game** built with **Unity**. You and a friend must work together, using separate perspectives and inputs, to solve interactive challenges and escape a series of locked rooms.

## ðŸŽ® Gameplay Overview

Escape Together is designed around **teamwork, communication, and synchronized puzzle-solving**.

* Two players share one screen using a **split-screen layout**.
* Each player explores their side of the room, interacting with objects, clues, locks, and mechanisms.
* Many puzzles require players to share information or perform actions simultaneously.
* Your goal: **escape the room together**.

* **Interactive objects** such as buttons, switches, levers, codes, and hidden mechanisms.
